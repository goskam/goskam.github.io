Features for Trainers & Studios

Own Your Platform

With your own branded mobile app, you're not at the mercy of third-party platforms or their unpredictable algorithms. You control how you interact with your clients, how your content is delivered, and how you grow your business. It’s your app, and your success is in your hands.

Updatable Calendar

Keep your clients up to date with an easily accessible, always-updated calendar of your yoga and fitness sessions. Add, modify, or cancel classes as needed, and your clients will always have the latest schedule at their fingertips.

Subscribed Video Content

Offer your clients exclusive access to a library of on-demand yoga and fitness videos. Your clients can subscribe to your video content and enjoy it anytime, anywhere, ensuring they never miss a session.

Favourite Videos

Allow your clients to save their favorite videos for quick access. Whether it's a specific yoga pose or a full class, they can easily revisit the content they love.

Customizable for Your Studio

Customize the app to reflect your brand, style, and unique offerings. Add your logo, update the interface, and tailor the features to your studio’s needs, ensuring the perfect fit for your business.

Grow Your User Base

With the ability to manage memberships, track progress, and engage with clients, this app helps you build and grow your user base, offering a seamless experience for both you and your clients.
